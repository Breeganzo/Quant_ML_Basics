**HQ:** New York  
**AUM:** ~$60+ billion

## Interview Process

### Typical Flow
1. Recruiter screen
2. Online coding assessment
3. Phone technical screen (1-2 rounds)
4. Virtual/Onsite (4-6 hours)
   - Coding
   - ML/Statistics
   - System design
   - Research discussion

## Sample Questions

1. Walk me through how you'd develop a trading strategy
2. How do you handle multiple comparisons in backtesting?
3. Design a data pipeline for tick data
4. Explain the difference between correlation and causation
5. How would you test if a signal is truly predictive?

## Tips for Success

1. **Research mindset**: Think like a scientist
2. **Explain clearly**: Communicate complex ideas simply
3. **Be rigorous**: Statistical significance matters
4. **Show curiosity**: Ask about their research
5. **Code cleanly**: Readable, tested code
