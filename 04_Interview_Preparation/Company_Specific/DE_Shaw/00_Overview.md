# DE Shaw Interview Preparation

## Company Overview

**Type:** Hedge fund + Technology  
**Founded:** 1988 (David E. Shaw)  
**HQ:** New York  
**AUM:** ~$60 billion  
**Employees:** ~2,000

## Culture

- Highly intellectual environment
- Research-driven
- Long-term thinking
- Diverse backgrounds welcome
- Emphasis on clear communication

## Interview Process

1. Phone screen (technical)
2. Superday (4-6 hours)
3. Focus on problem-solving ability

## Key Focus Areas

| Area | Weight |
|------|--------|
| Quantitative Ability | 30% |
| Coding | 25% |
| Statistics/ML | 25% |
| Communication | 20% |

## Essential Topics

- Probability and statistics
- Algorithm design
- Data structures
- Financial modeling
- Clear problem decomposition

## Tips

1. Think out loud
2. Structure your answers
3. Ask clarifying questions
4. Be prepared for open-ended problems
