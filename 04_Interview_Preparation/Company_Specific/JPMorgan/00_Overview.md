# JPMorgan Interview Preparation

## Company Overview

**Type:** Investment bank with quant divisions  
**Founded:** 2000 (merger)  
**HQ:** New York  
**Divisions:** Quantitative Research, Electronic Trading, Asset Management

## Key Divisions

### Quantitative Research
- Model development
- Risk analytics
- Pricing models

### Electronic Trading
- Execution algorithms
- Market making
- Low-latency systems

### Asset Management
- Factor investing
- Portfolio optimization
- Risk management

## Interview Process

1. HackerRank/Online assessment
2. Phone screens (1-2)
3. Superday (4-6 hours)

## Key Focus Areas

| Area | Weight |
|------|--------|
| Technical Skills | 35% |
| Coding | 25% |
| Market Knowledge | 20% |
| Behavioral | 20% |

## Essential Topics

- Time series analysis
- Risk modeling
- Credit risk
- Production systems
- Cloud platforms (AWS/Azure)
- Fixed income

## Tips

1. Know the division you're applying to
2. Understand JPMorgan's market position
3. Prepare behavioral examples
4. Show technical depth
