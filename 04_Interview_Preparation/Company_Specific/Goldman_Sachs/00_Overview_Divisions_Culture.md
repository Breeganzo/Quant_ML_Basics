# Goldman Sachs Interview Preparation

## Company Overview

**Type:** Investment bank with strong quant divisions  
**Founded:** 1869  
**HQ:** New York  
**Quant Divisions:** Strats, QIS, GSAM Quantitative, Electronic Trading

## Divisions

### Strats (Strategists)
- Support trading desks with quantitative models
- Pricing, risk, hedging strategies
- Close to trading floor

### QIS (Quantitative Investment Strategies)
- Systematic strategies
- Factor investing
- Multi-asset allocation

### GSAM Quantitative
- Asset management
- Factor models
- Risk parity strategies

### Electronic Trading
- Market making
- Execution algorithms
- Low-latency systems

## Interview Process

### Typical Flow
1. HackerRank coding test (1-2 hours)
2. Phone screen (1 hour)
3. Superday (4-6 hours)
   - Technical rounds
   - Behavioral (Goldman values)
   - Case studies

## Key Focus Areas

| Area | Weight | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| Quantitative Strategies | 25% | Mean reversion, momentum, stat arb |
| Risk Management | 25% | VaR, Greeks, stress testing |
| Coding (Python) | 25% | Clean, efficient, tested |
| Market Knowledge | 15% | Asset classes, current events |
| Behavioral | 10% | Goldman principles |

## Essential Topics

### Quantitative Strategies
- Mean reversion
- Momentum factors
- Statistical arbitrage
- Pairs trading
- Factor models (Fama-French)

### Risk Management
- Value at Risk (VaR)
- Expected Shortfall (CVaR)
- Greeks (delta, gamma, vega, theta)
- Stress testing
- Scenario analysis

### Portfolio Theory
- Markowitz optimization
- Risk parity
- Black-Litterman
- Tracking error

### Statistics
- Regression analysis
- Hypothesis testing
- Time series (ARIMA, GARCH)
- Correlation analysis

## Goldman Sachs Principles

Be prepared to discuss how you embody:
1. Client focus
2. Integrity
3. Excellence
4. Teamwork
5. Innovation

## Sample Questions

1. Design a pairs trading strategy. How do you select pairs?
2. Calculate VaR for a portfolio of options.
3. What factors drive equity returns?
4. How would you stress test a portfolio?
5. Explain the difference between historical and parametric VaR.

## Tips for Success

1. **Know the division**: Understand Strats vs QIS vs GSAM
2. **Market awareness**: Follow financial news
3. **Be structured**: Use frameworks for case questions
4. **Show leadership**: Have examples ready
5. **Technical depth**: Be ready to go deep on any topic
